About me

I am a Phd student at École Polytechnique de Montreal . Previously, I was an ASIC Verification engineer at Microsemi (Now MicroChip) where I worked on the next generation of OTN (Optical Transport Network) processors. And before that, I was a Software Engineer at Tru Simulation + Training.

My major areas of interests are:
  • Making Deep Neural Networks more computationally efficient
  • Deep Learning Acceleration

You can also check my C.V. or linkedin profile.
